Answer SHUTTLECLOCK

Written by Brian Chen, Jenna Himawan, and CJ Quines

On the meta page, there is a series of clocks. As more puzzles are solved, the clocks “light up” in order. The clocks read out times that are offset by a certain number of hours, minutes, and seconds from the user’s system time. The clocks are sorted in ascending order by offset.

The offsets, in order, are as follows.

  • 0:09:50
  • 3:35:00
  • 4:14:35
  • 6:20:50
  • 6:24:40
  • 6:50:05
  • 7:44:50
  • 8:50:10
  • 9:19:50
  • 11:05:20
  • 11:19:40
  • 11:24:40

The answers to the puzzles (in alphabetical order) are:

  • AMYWINEHOUSE
  • AQUEOUSHUMOR
  • DEUTSCHEMARK
  • EMBELLISHING
  • HALLEYSCOMET
  • IAMTHEWALRUS
  • MONOTHEISTIC
  • PHASEDIAGRAM
  • PHOTOREALISM
  • SOUTHAMERICA
  • STATEMACHINE
  • THUNDERSTORM

We notice that each answer has 12 letters and exactly 1 occurrence each of the letters H, M, and S, which correspond to “hour”, “minute”, and “second” on a clock.

By treating the first letter of the answer as “1” on a clock, the second letter as “2”, and so on, we can convert each answer to an arrangement of the hands on an analog clock.

(More precisely, the hand points somewhere between that number, inclusive, and the next number, exclusive. The second hand will be exactly on the number and the minute hand will be close, but the hour hand may be much closer to the next hand; this is for consistency with the next part.)
