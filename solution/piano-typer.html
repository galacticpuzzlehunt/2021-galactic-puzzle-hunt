Solution Piano Typer
Answer EARTH REBORN

Written by Amon Ge and Chris Jones
Implementation help by Jenna Himawan and Esther Wang
Art by sarawaleed

The puzzle is a rhythm and typing game where we type letters to the beat of bumpin’ classical remixes! The first song serves as a tutorial. The letters in this song tell us that we don’t need to otherwise use the letters of the songs, nor do we need to get a perfect score.

After we complete a song, we are given some rewards. Each of the reward notes/chords has a unique beat length. Ordering them from short to long spells the answer EARTH REBORN.

Beating the songs can be difficult. The best advice we have is to pick a difficult section, then play through the song repeatedly on no-fail mode and only play the difficult section (attempting to get it 100% correct). Just like on a real piano, this will help you develop muscle memory for the section.
